Deep Dive

Strategic Marketing Analysis

A rigorous competitive intelligence audit revealed significant whitespace opportunities that competitors were systematically ignoring in the KSA digital landscape. By mapping 12 direct competitors across Meta Ads Library, we identified critical gaps in offer positioning, creative angles, and audience targeting strategies.

The audit revealed that 80% of category advertisers were running commodity-level creative with price-first messaging — creating a massive opportunity to capture premium positioning through value-led narrative and aspirational storytelling.

  • Competitors spending 40%+ budget on top-of-funnel awareness with no retargeting architecture
  • Zero use of social proof mechanics or community-building assets in competitor creative
  • No seasonal or occasion-based campaign strategy identified across the competitive set
  • Critical gap identified in Arabic-first, culturally-resonant creative production
  • Premium audience segments (Saudis aged 25–44, high income) were completely underserved

Audience architecture was built from scratch using a proprietary three-layer segmentation model. By analyzing pixel data, CRM records, and behavioral patterns, we constructed precise audience segments that matched Alfa KSA's highest-value customer profiles.

The analysis revealed that the brand's true high-LTV customer was a Saudi female aged 28–40, urban, with disposable income above SAR 8,000/month — a segment that was completely absent from the brand's targeting strategy.

  • Primary: Saudi Females 25-44, Urban Centers (Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam), high intent signals
  • Secondary: Males 30-50, decision-makers, family-purchase behaviors, tech-forward
  • Tertiary: Expats in KSA, 25-45, English-Arabic bilingual, premium lifestyle interest
  • Custom audiences built from website visitors, video viewers (75%+), and purchaser lookalikes
  • Exclusion layers applied to reduce ad fatigue and prevent cannibalizing bottom-funnel audiences

A complete audit of the customer journey revealed a fundamentally broken conversion architecture. Users were encountering the brand for the first time and immediately being asked to purchase — a strategy that ignores all established consumer psychology principles.

We redesigned the entire funnel from the ground up using a five-stage model: Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Intent → Conversion, with tailored creative, messaging, and targeting at each stage.

  • Awareness stage: Video content driving reach, brand story, social proof establishment
  • Interest stage: Carousel showcasing product benefits, UGC testimonials, lifestyle imagery
  • Consideration stage: Comparison content, detailed product demonstrations, trust signals
  • Intent stage: Special offer retargeting, limited availability messaging, urgency drivers
  • Conversion stage: Frictionless checkout path, abandoned cart sequences, post-purchase upsell

The Blueprint

Marketing Strategy Implemented

A multi-dimensional, data-driven strategy built for scale — every element engineered to compound performance and create sustainable, defensible growth.

01
Paid Ads Architecture & Meta Ads Mastery
Completely rebuilt the Meta Ads campaign structure using a Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO) model with a three-tiered funnel approach. Implemented Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns for eCommerce scaling alongside manual Reach & Frequency buying for brand awareness phases. Dynamic creative testing ran continuously with 40+ creative variants active at peak.
Meta Ads CBO A+ Shopping Dynamic Creatives
02
Conversion Funnel Engineering
Architected a five-stage conversion funnel with stage-specific creative, messaging, and bidding strategies. Landing pages were A/B tested with heatmap analysis driving iterative improvements. Mobile-first design was enforced across all touchpoints, with checkout friction reduced by removing 6 unnecessary form fields.
Funnel Design CRO A/B Testing UX Optimization
03
Precision Retargeting Architecture
Built a 7-layer retargeting ecosystem covering: website visitors (30/60/90 day windows), video viewers (25%, 50%, 75%, 95% thresholds), add-to-cart abandoners, checkout abandoners, purchaser upsell sequences, and cross-sell campaigns to existing customers based on purchase history patterns.
Retargeting Custom Audiences Pixel Events Upsell Flows
04
Creative Direction & Production Strategy
Established a systematic creative production pipeline delivering 15–20 new creative assets monthly. Video-first strategy with Arabic voiceover, culturally-relevant storylines, and platform-native formats (Reels, Stories, Carousels). UGC-style creative production reduced costs by 44% while improving authentic engagement metrics.
Creative Direction Video Production UGC Strategy Arabic Creative

Budget Allocation & Scaling Methodology
Implemented a performance-gated scaling protocol: budget increases only triggered when ROAS targets were consistently hit for 5+ consecutive days. Scaling velocity was capped at 20% weekly to protect algorithm learning phases. Budget distribution: 40% cold audiences, 30% warm retargeting, 20% lookalikes, 10% testing.

Strategic Wisdom

Lessons Learned

Creative Strategy
"Format follows function — Reels outperformed every other format by 40% once we invested in mobile-native, Arabic-first production."
Actionable Takeaway: Always test platform-native formats before assuming creative hierarchies. The Meta algorithm rewards content that feels organic, not promotional.
Audience Architecture
"Exclusion audiences were as valuable as targeting audiences — stopping spend on saturated cold audiences unlocked 28% efficiency gains overnight."
Actionable Takeaway: Build comprehensive exclusion layers before scaling. Most brands are paying to reach people they've already converted or permanently disqualified.
Scaling Discipline
"Patience in scaling is a competitive advantage — brands that rush budget increases destroy the very algorithm performance they've built."
Actionable Takeaway: Implement performance gates for all budget increases. A 20% weekly cap, triggered only by 5-day ROAS consistency, compounds gains rather than disrupting them.
Market Intelligence
"The KSA market rewards cultural authenticity above all else — generic global creative consistently underperforms locally-crafted narrative by 58%."
Actionable Takeaway: Always create market-specific creative assets. Cultural intelligence is not a nice-to-have — it's a fundamental performance variable in regional markets.
Funnel Design
"Removing 6 unnecessary checkout fields increased conversion rate by 1.4 percentage points — simplicity is the ultimate conversion tool."
Actionable Takeaway: Audit your checkout process with mobile users as the primary test subject. Every additional click, field, and decision point costs you measurable conversion rate.
Brand Building
"Brand equity is a ROAS multiplier — every 10% improvement in brand perception score reduced CPC by approximately 8% through improved relevance signals."
Actionable Takeaway: Brand building is not separate from performance marketing — it's the foundation that makes performance marketing progressively cheaper and more effective over time.
